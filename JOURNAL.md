### Question: Why?

Now Lets Start the actual work here.
Firstly, lets answer the main question here; Why the Hell I'm making a mouse in the first place?  

Answer is very simple and clear:  
 I have a Gaming Mouse which I bought after I saw it in my friend's place, I really loved it... Then I asked him where did you get it from? I went to that place and Found it. After one year of using it, I encountered an issue, The mouse was RGB and it had no Software nor any option on the hardware side to change the RGB neon lightning effect, that always pisses me off. I really love this mouse build quality and core performance. But No control on the RGB was pain in the rear.

### Problem:

I got a visual representation of my mouse's RGB LEDs Changing effect: Its in gif image formate, Hope your able to View it:

![](https://github.com/lassiipk/saim-mias/blob/main/Assets/Images/Mouse_RGB-Effect/202607210249_1080x1080.gif)

Anyways lets continue discussing the project,  After some research on my mouse i found out that my mouse is not capable of doing such action, also know as ARGB, It stands for Addressable Red Green Blue lightening effect. the core problem was with the microcontroller and non-ARGB lights. This was all figured and concluded after opening the mouse to view its internal components, I Also have some images to display the problem, I'll make sure to show them for you to look into it if you are interested init.
